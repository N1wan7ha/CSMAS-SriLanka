Welcome to CSMAS
Catholic Social Media Admins of Sri Lanka

CSMAS is a vibrant community of Catholic student leaders dedicated to spreading the message of Christ through social media. As digital missionaries, we combine creativity, faith, and technology to make Christ known in the digital world.

Discover Our Mission

Who We Are

We are Catholic students from across Sri Lanka, entrusted with managing official social media pages of parishes, dioceses, ministries, and Catholic organizations. Together, we form a vibrant digital apostolate, collaborating to share God’s Word and inspire our followers through engaging, responsible, and spirit-filled online content.


Our Mission

“To evangelize the digital world through faith-filled content, teamwork, and Catholic values.”

Our mission is to uphold and share the Gospel through modern communication tools. We aim to educate, inspire, and connect with people online while reflecting the Catholic Church’s values in everything we post and share.


What We Do

  • Manage and promote Catholic events and liturgies online
  • Create and curate visual content that reflects the beauty of our faith
  • Unite Catholic digital admins to support each other and grow spiritually
  • Promote the teachings of the Catholic Church through respectful dialogue
  • Conduct workshops, webinars, and discussions to improve digital evangelization

Why It Matters

Social media is the new mission field. As Catholic content creators, we recognize the responsibility and power of our platforms. Through collaboration and prayer, CSMAS brings together those who serve behind the screens ensuring that the digital space remains a place of truth, love, and hope.
